How it works

DuskScreen is designed with a Privacy-First architecture. Unlike traditional platforms that sync your sensitive data to remote servers, DuskScreen operates entirely within your local environment.

Data Persistence

Uses browser-based localStorage. No database accounts, no passwords, no risk of data breaches.

Identity-Free

Zero login required. Your device is your identity. Settings are locked to this specific browser instance.
